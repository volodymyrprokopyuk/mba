\startlines
{\bf Gross revenue}
- Goods costs (direct costs)
= {\bf Gross profit}
- Operating expenses (indirect costs)
- Depreciation expenses (amortización)
= {\bf Operating profit}, EBIT (Earnings Before Interest and Taxes)
+ Interest income
- Interest expenses
= {\bf Profit before taxes}
- Taxes (impuestos)
= {\bf Net profit} (over a period)
